A Sandbox initiative delving into topics like personal growth, mental health and self-care.

Project

Gone are the days of the classroom but for many of us, the monotony of everyday life remains. Add on the increasing pressure to appear successful and happy in the realm of social media and it seems as though there aren’t many outlets for us to truly find the break we’re looking for.

We’ve created the Recess series with this in mind. Those who continue acquiring new skills into adulthood report better stress-management skills, self-esteem, positivity, and feelings of purpose.

Our goal is to create a community of openness and curiosity, free of judgement and full of possibilities.
