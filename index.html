The Project

Gone are the days of the classroom but for many of us, the monotony of everyday life remains.


Add on the increasing pressure to appear successful and happy in the realm of social media and it seems as though there aren’t many outlets for us to truly find the break we’re looking for.

We’ve created the Recess series with this in mind. Those who continue acquiring new skills into adulthood report better stress-management skills, self-esteem, positivity, and feelings of purpose.

Our goal is to create a community of openness and curiosity, free of judgement and full of possibilities.

The Event

Oct 20th 2016 | 6:30pm - 8:30pm

Lululemon Athletica | 318 Queen St. W.

The first instalment in the Recess series is an open discussion with like-minded people who will be able to speak on how learning a new skill changed the trajectory of their lives for the better. Both our guest speakers and our audience can share their stories and connect in this intimate space.
